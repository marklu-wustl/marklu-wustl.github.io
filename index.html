Zeyu (Mark) Lu

First‑year Ph.D. Student · Washington University in St. Louis

My research lies at the intersection of cognitive science and machine learning. By creating novel cognitive assessment platforms that capture the entire behavioral trajectory and integrate multimodal physiological signals, I preserve the rigor of psychometric testing while more effectively revealing individualized cognitive profiles, opening new avenues for clinical diagnosis, education, and human–machine interaction.
